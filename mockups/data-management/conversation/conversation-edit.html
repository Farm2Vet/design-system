Turn 12 · editing
Role: Veterinarian

Based on your description, the piglets are most likely suffering from Transmissible Gastroenteritis (TGE) — a coronavirus. Recommended actions:

Supportive care. Provide oral electrolytes and glucose solutions to affected piglets to combat dehydration.

Biosecurity. Isolate affected piglets. Disinfect pens with virucidal agents.

Veterinary care. Consult an on-site veterinarian for confirmation by PCR and a tailored treatment plan.

Supporting points

4 points
Age (3–5 days) and morbidity (8/12) suggest a transmissible neonatal enteric pathogen.
Sow has been vaccinated against E. coli — reducing the probability of colibacillosis.
Yellow watery diarrhea + vomiting + rapid weight loss is a textbook TGE presentation.
Antibiotics alone won't resolve a viral etiology — supportive care is first-line.
