Sync disease data to chatbot

Rebuilds the chatbot's retrieval embeddings from all active diseases.

This integrates the latest live versions into the assistant's knowledge base. 42 active diseases will be embedded; 5 incomplete or inactive diseases will be skipped.

i
Embedding usually completes in under a minute. The current chatbot stays live until the new index is ready.

Deactivate this disease?

It has a live version the chatbot is currently using.

Deactivating removes this disease from the chatbot's answers on the next sync. The content is kept and can be reactivated at any time.

Delete disease?

This permanently removes the disease and all of its versions.

Deleting removes all versions and both languages from the knowledge base. This cannot be undone.
